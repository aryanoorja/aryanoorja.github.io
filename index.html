About Us

Oorja Threads is a personal passion project by Me, based in Bhopal. Every bracelet is handcrafted with care and attention to detail, designed to add a unique touch to your everyday style. Quality and creativity are at the heart of everything I make.

I’m Aryan , the creator and maker behind Oorja Threads. This is a one-person venture where I personally design and handcraft each bracelet with focus on quality, creativity, and style. My goal is to offer accessories that stand out and complement your personal look effortlessly.

Whether you’re looking for something casual or a bit speacial, each piece is made to be durable, comfortable, and unique I’m constantly experimenting with new ideas and materials to bring fresh designs to life.

Thank you for visiting and supporting this journey. I hope you find something here that speaks to you!
